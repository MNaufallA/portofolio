About Me

I am a Biomedical Engineering fresh graduate from Airlangga University with academic and internship experience in medical instrumentation, electronics, and machine learning. During my studies and internships in Indonesia and Taiwan, I gained hands-on exposure to projects including the development of health monitoring devices, the application of AI for healthcare prediction, and the creation of web-based systems for medical device automation.
